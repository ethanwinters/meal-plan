Burgers

Serves 220 min
Weeks 1 & 3 — Saturday

Ingredients

  • Ground beef — 1 lb
  • Burger buns — 2
  • Shredded or sliced cheddar
  • Romaine or iceberg lettuce
  • Roma tomatoes, sliced
  • Condiments
  • Side — see weekly variation

Steps

  1. Divide beef into 2 patties. Season generously with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Grill over high heat: 3–4 min per side for medium.
  3. Add cheese in the last minute. Close the lid to melt.
  4. Toast buns cut-side down on the grill, 1 min.
  5. Build and serve with the week's side.

Weekly Variations

Week 1Grilled asparagus — toss in olive oil, grill over high heat 4–5 min, finish with lemon
Week 3Smash-style: thin patties on a screaming hot cast iron, brioche buns, arugula salad with Newman's Own lemon vinaigrette
