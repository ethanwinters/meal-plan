Gochujang Chicken Thighs

Serves 2 for dinner + preps 3 Korean quinoa bowl lunches35 min active
Week 3 — Sunday (Prep Day)

Ingredients

  • Bone-in skin-on chicken thighs — 4–6 (double batch)
  • Gochujang — 2 tbsp
  • Soy sauce — 2 tbsp
  • Rice vinegar — 1 tbsp
  • Garlic — 3 cloves, minced
  • Fresh ginger — 1 tsp, grated
  • Lime juice — 1 lime
  • Brown sugar — 1 tsp

Steps

  1. Whisk together gochujang, soy, rice vinegar, garlic, ginger, lime juice, and brown sugar.
  2. Coat thighs and marinate at least 30 min (up to overnight).
  3. Air fry at 400°F for 22–25 min, skin-side up. The gochujang will caramelize — watch for any burning and drop to 380°F if needed.
  4. Rest 5 min, then slice.
  5. Serve over quinoa with edamame, cucumber, scallion, and dressing for dinner.
  6. Refrigerate extra sliced chicken for quinoa bowls.
No sesame oil in this house — the ginger-soy dressing (soy sauce, rice vinegar, garlic, ginger, lime) carries the bowl just fine without it. Gochujang heat level varies by brand; taste the marinade before committing.
