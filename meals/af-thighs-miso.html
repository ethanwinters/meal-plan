Air Fryer Chicken Thighs + Roasted Broccoli

Serves 2 for dinner + preps 3 bowl lunches40 min total
Week 4 — Sunday (Prep Day)

Ingredients

  • Bone-in skin-on chicken thighs — 4
  • White miso paste — 2 tbsp
  • Soy sauce — 1 tbsp
  • Rice vinegar — 1 tbsp
  • Fresh ginger — 1 tsp, grated
  • Garlic — 2 cloves, minced
  • Broccoli — 2 large heads, cut into florets
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk together miso, soy, rice vinegar, ginger, and garlic. Coat thighs and marinate 30+ min.
  2. Air fry at 400°F for 22–25 min, skin-side up, until deeply golden and cooked through.
  3. Toss broccoli in olive oil, salt, pepper. Roast at 425°F for 20–25 min until edges char.
  4. Serve 1 thigh each with half the broccoli, edamame, and cucumber for dinner.
  5. Refrigerate remaining thighs (sliced) and broccoli for bowls.
Miso burns faster than plain marinade — if the skin gets dark before time is up, drop the temp to 375°F for the last 5 min.
