Pork Tenderloin + Arugula Salad

Serves 230 min
Week 4 — Saturday

Ingredients

  • Pork tenderloin — 1–1.25 lbs
  • Garlic — 3 cloves, minced
  • Fresh rosemary — 1 sprig, chopped
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper
  • Arugula — 4 cups
  • Newman's Own lemon vinaigrette

Steps

  1. Mix olive oil, garlic, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Rub all over the tenderloin.
  2. Air fry at 400°F for 20–22 min, flipping halfway, until internal temp hits 145°F.
  3. Rest 5–10 min before slicing — this is non-negotiable for pork tenderloin.
  4. Toss arugula with lemon vinaigrette and serve alongside.
Pull it at 145°F — pork tenderloin goes from perfect to dry very fast. The rest time matters here more than almost anything else.
