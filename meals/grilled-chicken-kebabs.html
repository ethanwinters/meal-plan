Grilled Chicken Kebabs + Grilled Veg

Serves 2 for dinner + preps 3 quinoa bowl lunches45 min total
Week 2 — Sunday (Prep Day)

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts — 2, cubed into 1.5" pieces
  • Zucchini — 2
  • Cherry tomatoes — 1 pint
  • Red onion — 1
  • Garlic — 4 cloves
  • Olive oil, smoked paprika, salt, pepper
  • Skewers

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken cubes in olive oil, garlic, paprika, salt, pepper — 30+ min.
  2. Thread chicken onto skewers.
  3. Grill kebabs over medium-high, turning every 3–4 min, about 12–14 min total.
  4. Grill zucchini (halved), whole cherry tomatoes in a grill basket, and onion rings until charred.
  5. Serve half the veg alongside kebabs for dinner.
  6. Reserve other half for quinoa bowls.
If using wooden skewers, soak in water 30 min first. Metal skewers are easier.
