Air Fryer Chicken Thighs + Roasted Veg

Serves 2 for dinner + preps 3 quinoa bowl lunches40 min total
Week 1 — Sunday (Prep Day)

Ingredients

  • Bone-in skin-on chicken thighs — 4
  • Zucchini — 2, cut into chunks
  • Bell peppers — 2, cut into chunks
  • Red onion — 1, cut into wedges
  • Lemon — 1
  • Fresh herbs — parsley, thyme
  • Olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Marinate thighs in olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, herbs, salt, pepper — 30+ min (overnight is better).
  2. Air fry at 400°F for 22–25 min, skin-side up, until skin is crisp and internal temp hits 175°F.
  3. Toss veg in olive oil, salt, pepper. Roast on a sheet pan at 425°F for 25–30 min.
  4. Serve 1 thigh each with half the veg for dinner.
  5. Refrigerate remaining thighs (sliced) and veg for quinoa bowls.
Thighs are much more forgiving than breasts — internal temp of 175°F+ is fine and keeps them juicy. Let them rest 5 min before slicing.
