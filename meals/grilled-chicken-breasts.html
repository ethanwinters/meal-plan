Grilled Chicken Breasts + Grilled Veg

Serves 2 for dinner + preps 3 quinoa bowl lunches45 min total
Week 3 — Sunday (Prep Day)

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts — 2, pounded to even ¾" thickness
  • Eggplant — 1 large, sliced
  • Bell peppers — 2, quartered
  • Red onion — 1
  • Lemon — 1
  • Fresh herbs — parsley, thyme
  • Olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Pound chicken to even ¾" thickness — critical for even cooking.
  2. Marinate in olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, herbs — 30+ min.
  3. Grill chicken over medium: 5–6 min per side. Do not overcook — pull at 160°F.
  4. Grill eggplant slices, pepper quarters, and onion rings until softened and charred.
  5. Serve half the veg with chicken for dinner.
  6. Reserve other half for quinoa bowls.
Chicken breasts dry out fast. Pounding them even and not going past 165°F is the whole game.
