Air Fryer Eggplant Parm

Serves 235 min
Week 4 — Monday

Ingredients

  • Eggplant — 1 medium, sliced into ½" rounds
  • Panko breadcrumbs — ½ cup
  • Egg — 1, beaten
  • Marinara — ½ cup
  • Fresh mozzarella — 4 oz
  • Arugula — 2 cups
  • Lemon — ½
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper
  • Baby spinach — 2 cups
  • Italian dressing

Steps

  1. Salt eggplant slices generously. Let sit 10 min, then pat completely dry.
  2. Dip in egg, then panko. Season.
  3. Air fry at 400°F for 12–15 min, flipping halfway, until golden and tender.
  4. Top with marinara and mozzarella. Air fry 2 more min.
  5. Dress arugula with lemon juice and olive oil. Serve alongside.
Salting and patting dry is the key step — eggplant holds a lot of water and will steam instead of crisp if you skip it.
