Turkey Meatballs + Spaghetti

Serves 220 min (meatballs pre-made)
Weeks 1 & 3 — Monday

Ingredients

  • Turkey meatballs — from freezer
  • Spaghetti — 6 oz dry
  • Jarred marinara — 1½ cups
  • Parmesan — freshly grated
  • Baby spinach — 2 cups
  • Italian dressing

Steps

  1. Pull meatballs from freezer in the morning to thaw.
  2. Warm meatballs in marinara over medium-low, covered, 15 min.
  3. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water per package.
  4. Chop romaine and dress with Italian dressing.
  5. Drain pasta. Serve with sauce, meatballs, and parmesan.
If meatballs are still frozen, add 10 min to the simmer. Use a lid so they steam through.
