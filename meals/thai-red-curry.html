Thai Red Curry Chicken

Serves 225 min
Week 3 — Thursday

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs, boneless — 1 lb, cubed
  • Red curry paste — 2 tbsp
  • Coconut milk, full fat — 1 can
  • Leftover Wed roasted sweet potato & green beans
  • Fish sauce — 1 tbsp
  • Lime juice — 1 tbsp
  • Jasmine rice — ¾ cup dry

Steps

  1. Cook jasmine rice per package.
  2. Heat oil. Fry curry paste 1 min until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken, cook 4–5 min.
  4. Pour in coconut milk. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Add leftover sweet potato and green beans to warm through.
  6. Season with fish sauce and lime juice. Serve over jasmine rice.
Red curry is richer and slightly more heat than green. Sweet potato holds up well in the sauce and makes it a bit more filling.
