Teriyaki Chicken Breast + Broccolini

Serves 2 for dinner + batch-cooks thighs for 3 bowl lunches40 min + marinating
Week 2 — Sunday (Prep Day)

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts, boneless skinless — 2 (dinner)
  • Bone-in skin-on chicken thighs — 4 (batch-cooked for lunch bowls)
  • — Teriyaki marinade —
  • Soy sauce — ¼ cup
  • Honey — 2 tbsp
  • Garlic — 3 cloves, minced
  • Fresh ginger — 1 tsp, grated
  • Rice vinegar — 1 tbsp
  • — Dinner side —
  • Broccolini — 2–3 bunches, halved
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper
  • — Lunch bowls —
  • Persian cucumber — sliced
  • Scallions — sliced

Steps

  1. Mix soy sauce, honey, garlic, ginger, and rice vinegar. Coat the breasts and the thighs and marinate in the fridge (Sunday morning works great — 8–10 hrs is ideal).
  2. Air fry the breasts at 375°F for 16–20 min, flipping once, to 160–165°F. Honey browns fast — watch the last few minutes. Rest 5 min.
  3. Air fry the thighs at 400°F for 22–25 min, skin-side up, to 175°F — these go to the lunch bowls.
  4. Toss broccolini halves in olive oil, salt, pepper. Roast cut-side up at 425°F for 10–12 min until edges char.
  5. Dinner: a breast each with the broccolini.
  6. Slice the thighs and refrigerate for the teriyaki lunch bowls.
Don't use leftover marinade as a sauce — it had raw chicken in it. Set a little aside before marinating if you want extra for the bowls. Breast dries out sooner than thigh — pull it at 160°F.
