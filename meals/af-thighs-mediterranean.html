Air Fryer Chicken Thighs + Roasted Veg

Serves 2 for dinner + preps 3 rice bowl lunches40 min total
Week 2 — Sunday (Prep Day)

Ingredients

  • Bone-in skin-on chicken thighs — 4
  • Cherry tomatoes — 1 pint
  • Zucchini — 2, cut into chunks
  • Red onion — 1, cut into wedges
  • Lemon — 1
  • Dried oregano — 1 tsp
  • Olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper
  • Kalamata olives
  • Crumbled feta

Steps

  1. Marinate thighs in olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, pepper — 30+ min.
  2. Air fry at 400°F for 22–25 min, skin-side up, until skin is crisp and internal temp hits 175°F.
  3. Toss cherry tomatoes, zucchini, and onion in olive oil, salt, pepper. Roast at 425°F for 25 min.
  4. Serve 1 thigh each with half the veg, some olives, and feta for dinner.
  5. Refrigerate remaining thighs (sliced), veg, olives, and feta for rice bowls.
The roasted cherry tomatoes get jammy and sweet — they're the best part of the rice bowls.
