Grilled Turkey Kielbasa + Grilled Veg

Serves 2 for dinner + preps 3 quinoa bowl lunches40 min total
Week 4 — Sunday (Prep Day)

Ingredients

  • Turkey kielbasa — 1 package (12–14 oz)
  • Asparagus — 1 bunch, trimmed
  • Zucchini — 2, halved
  • Cherry tomatoes — 1 pint
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper, lemon

Steps

  1. Kielbasa is pre-cooked — you're just charring the outside.
  2. Grill over high heat, turning every 2–3 min, about 8–10 min total. Look for a deep char on the casing.
  3. Toss asparagus in olive oil, grill in a single layer 4–5 min.
  4. Grill zucchini (cut side down) and cherry tomatoes (grill basket) until charred.
  5. Slice kielbasa on the bias. Serve with half the veg.
  6. Reserve other half for quinoa bowls.
Turkey kielbasa grills faster than beef — watch for flare-ups from the casing fat.
