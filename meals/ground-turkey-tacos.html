Ground Turkey Tacos

Serves 220 min
Every Tuesday

Ingredients

  • Ground turkey — 1 lb
  • Taco seasoning — 1 packet
  • Flour or corn tortillas
  • Shredded cheddar
  • Baby spinach
  • Roma tomatoes, diced
  • Plain Greek yogurt
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Brown ground turkey over medium-high, breaking into small pieces.
  2. Drain excess liquid. Add taco seasoning and ⅓ cup water. Simmer 3–4 min.
  3. Warm tortillas.
  4. Build: turkey → cheddar → lettuce → tomato → Greek yogurt → salsa.
Greek yogurt works perfectly as a sour cream substitute here. Classic American tacos, every Tuesday — no variation needed.
