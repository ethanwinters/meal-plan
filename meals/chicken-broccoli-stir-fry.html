Chicken & Broccoli Stir Fry

Serves 220 min
Week 4 — Thursday

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs, boneless — 1 lb, thinly sliced
  • Leftover Wed roasted broccoli
  • Oyster sauce — 3 tbsp
  • Soy sauce — 2 tbsp
  • Fresh ginger — 1 tsp, grated
  • Garlic — 3 cloves, minced
  • Chicken broth — ¼ cup
  • Cornstarch — 1 tsp
  • Brown rice — ¾ cup dry

Steps

  1. Cook brown rice per package.
  2. Whisk together oyster sauce, soy, broth, and cornstarch.
  3. Heat wok over high. Add oil. Stir fry chicken until cooked, about 4 min. Remove.
  4. Add garlic and ginger. Cook 30 seconds.
  5. Add leftover broccoli. Toss to heat through.
  6. Return chicken. Pour sauce over everything. Toss until sauce thickens, about 1 min.
  7. Serve over brown rice.
No sesame oil or sesame seeds in this recipe — Charlie's allergy. The oyster and soy combination carries plenty of flavor without it.
