Air Fryer Chicken Parm

Serves 230 min
Week 2 — Monday

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts — 2, pounded to ½" even thickness
  • Panko breadcrumbs — ½ cup
  • Egg — 1, beaten
  • Marinara — ½ cup
  • Fresh mozzarella — 4 oz
  • Fresh spinach — large handful
  • Olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Pound chicken to even ½" thickness.
  2. Dip in egg, then panko. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Air fry at 375°F for 10–12 min, flipping halfway.
  4. Top with marinara and mozzarella. Air fry 2 more min until cheese melts.
  5. Sauté spinach in olive oil and garlic while chicken rests.
Don't overcrowd the basket. A light oil spray on the panko keeps it crispier.
