BBQ Chicken + Grilled Corn

Serves 230 min
Week 2 — Saturday

Ingredients

  • Bone-in skin-on chicken thighs — 4
  • BBQ sauce — ½ cup (check label: no sesame)
  • Corn on the cob — 2 ears
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Season thighs with salt and pepper. Grill skin-side down over medium heat, 10–12 min.
  2. Flip and brush generously with BBQ sauce. Grill another 8–10 min, saucing once more, until internal temp hits 175°F.
  3. Grill corn alongside, turning every 2–3 min, about 10 min total.
  4. Let chicken rest 5 min before serving.
Most major BBQ sauce brands (Sweet Baby Ray's, KC Masterpiece) are sesame-free, but check the label — some specialty sauces are not.
