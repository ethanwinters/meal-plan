Italian Chicken Sausage + Pasta

Serves 225 min
Weeks 2 & 4 — Monday

Ingredients

  • Italian chicken sausage links — 3–4 (about 12 oz)
  • Jarred marinara — 1½ cups
  • Penne or rigatoni — 6 oz dry
  • Parmesan — freshly grated
  • Baby spinach — 2 cups
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Italian dressing

Steps

  1. Brown sausage links in a skillet over medium, turning, about 10–12 min until cooked through. Slice on the bias.
  2. Warm marinara in the same pan.
  3. Cook pasta in salted boiling water per package.
  4. Toss spinach, cherry tomatoes, and cucumber with Italian dressing.
  5. Drain pasta. Plate with sauce, sausage slices, and parmesan.
Chicken sausage dries out fast — pull it off heat as soon as it hits 165°F. Slicing on the bias gives more surface area for the sauce.
