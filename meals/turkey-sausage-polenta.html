Turkey Sausage + Polenta

Serves 235 min
Week 3 — Monday

Ingredients

  • Turkey Italian sausage — 3 links (about 12 oz)
  • Bell peppers — 2, sliced
  • Yellow onion — 1, sliced
  • Polenta — 1 tube or ¾ cup dry
  • Chicken broth — 2 cups (if using dry)
  • Parmesan — ¼ cup grated
  • Butter — 1 tbsp
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Roast sliced peppers and onion at 425°F for 20 min, tossing once.
  2. Brown sausage links in a skillet, turning to char all sides, about 12 min. Slice on the bias.
  3. Tube polenta: slice and pan-fry in butter until golden. Dry polenta: cook in broth per package.
  4. Finish polenta with parmesan and a knob of butter.
  5. Plate polenta, top with sausage and roasted peppers.
