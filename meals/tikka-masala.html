Chicken Tikka Masala

Serves 225 min
Week 2 — Thursday

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs, boneless — 1 lb, cubed
  • Tikka masala sauce — 1 jar (Patak's or Trader Joe's)
  • Leftover Wed roasted veg
  • Frozen riced cauliflower — 1 bag
  • Fresh cilantro (optional)

Steps

  1. Brown chicken in oil over medium-high, about 5 min.
  2. Pour in tikka masala sauce. Simmer 10–12 min until chicken is cooked through.
  3. Stir in leftover roasted veg to warm through.
  4. Microwave frozen cauliflower rice per package.
  5. Serve curry over cauliflower rice. Top with cilantro if using.
Jarred tikka masala is completely fine — Patak's and Trader Joe's are both solid. No need to make it from scratch on a Thursday.
