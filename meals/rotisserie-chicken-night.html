Rotisserie Chicken Night

Serves 225 min active (just roasting veg)
Every Wednesday

Ingredients

  • Rotisserie chicken — 1 (picked up from store)
  • Veg (see weekly variation below) — enough for triple batch
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Pick up rotisserie chicken on the way home.
  2. Cut veg into bite-sized pieces. Toss with olive oil, salt, pepper.
  3. Spread across TWO large sheet pans — you're roasting a triple batch.
  4. Roast at 425°F for 20–25 min, tossing halfway, until caramelized.
  5. Plate ⅓ of the veg as tonight's side with the rotisserie chicken.
  6. Pack remaining veg into two separate containers. Refrigerate.
The triple-roast is the engine of the system. One 25-min oven session produces: Wednesday dinner, Thu/Fri pasta salad lunches, and Thursday dinner. Use both sheet pans or the veg steams instead of roasting.

Weekly Variations

Week 1Zucchini, bell pepper, red onion → ⅓ to Thu green curry
Week 2Mixed veggies → ⅓ to Thu tikka masala
Week 3Sweet potatoes, green beans → ⅓ to Thu red curry
Week 4Broccoli → ⅓ to Thu stir fry
