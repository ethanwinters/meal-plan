Thai Green Curry Chicken

Serves 215 min
Week 1 — Thursday

Ingredients

  • Leftover rotisserie chicken — pulled or chopped, about 2 cups
  • Green curry paste — 2 tbsp
  • Coconut milk, full fat — 1 can (13.5 oz)
  • Leftover Wed roasted veg (zucchini, bell pepper, onion)
  • Fish sauce — 1 tbsp
  • Lime juice — 1 tbsp
  • Frozen riced cauliflower — 1 bag

Steps

  1. Pull or chop the leftover rotisserie chicken. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or deep skillet over medium.
  3. Fry curry paste 1 min, stirring, until fragrant.
  4. Pour in coconut milk. Bring to a gentle simmer.
  5. Add chicken and leftover roasted veg — everything is already cooked, just heat through, 2–3 min.
  6. Season with fish sauce and lime juice.
  7. Microwave frozen cauliflower rice per package. Serve curry on top.
Since both the chicken and veg are already cooked, this is a 15-minute dinner. Don't let it simmer too long or the veg turns to mush — just enough to heat everything through.
