Herb-Lemon Chicken Breast + Roasted Veg

Serves 2 for dinner + batch-cooks thighs for 3 quinoa bowl lunches40 min total
Weeks 1 & 3 — Sunday (Prep Day)

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts, boneless skinless — 2 (dinner)
  • Bone-in skin-on chicken thighs — 4 (batch-cooked for lunch bowls)
  • Zucchini — 2, cut into chunks
  • Bell peppers — 2, cut into chunks
  • Red onion — 1, cut into wedges
  • Lemon — 1
  • Fresh herbs — parsley, thyme
  • Olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Make the marinade: olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, chopped herbs, salt, pepper. Coat the breasts and the thighs (in the rest of it). Marinate 30+ min — overnight is better.
  2. Air fry the breasts at 375°F for 16–20 min, flipping once, until internal temp hits 160–165°F. Rest 5 min — they carry over to 165°F.
  3. Air fry the thighs at 400°F for 22–25 min, skin-side up, to 175°F. These are for the week's lunch bowls, not dinner.
  4. Toss veg in olive oil, salt, pepper. Roast a big batch on a sheet pan at 425°F for 25–30 min — enough for dinner plus the bowls.
  5. Dinner: a breast each with half the roasted veg.
  6. Slice the thighs and refrigerate with the remaining veg for quinoa bowls.
Keeping the thighs for the bowls is the whole point — dinner (breast) and lunches (thighs) are no longer the same dish twice. Breast is leaner and far less forgiving than thigh: pull it at 160°F and let it rest, or it dries out. Two cuts means staggering the air fryer (breasts, then thighs) while the veg roasts in the oven.
