Grilled Chicken Thighs + Grilled Veg

Serves 2 for dinner + preps 3 quinoa bowl lunches45 min total
Week 1 — Sunday (Prep Day)

Ingredients

  • Bone-in chicken thighs — 4 pieces
  • Zucchini — 3, halved lengthwise
  • Bell peppers — 2, quartered
  • Red onion — 1, cut into thick rings
  • Lemon — 1
  • Garlic — 4 cloves
  • Fresh herbs — thyme or rosemary
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Marinate thighs in olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and herbs — at least 30 min, up to overnight.
  2. Grill chicken over medium heat: 6–8 min per side until cooked through (165°F internal).
  3. Grill veg in batches over high heat: 4–5 min per side for good char.
  4. Serve half the veg alongside chicken for dinner.
  5. Set other half aside to cool. Goes into quinoa bowls (see Sunday Prep on the week page).
Bone-in thighs are very forgiving on the grill — harder to dry out than breasts. The char on the veg is flavor, not a mistake.
