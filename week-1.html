Sunday Prep
Air fryer + oven

Chicken thighs (herb & lemon) + oven-roast batch: zucchini, bell pepper, red onion

Half the veg + one thigh each is dinner. Rest goes in the fridge for quinoa bowls.
Herb-lemon quinoa bowls — Mon · Tue · Wed lunches
  • 1½ cups dry quinoa — cook and cool
  • Air-fried chicken thighs — sliced
  • Roasted zucchini, bell pepper, red onion
  • Cannellini beans — 1 can, drained
  • Lemon-olive oil vinaigrette

Divide into 3 containers. Good for 3 days refrigerated.

Turkey meatballs — double batch (freezer)
  • Ground turkey — 2 lbs
  • Breadcrumbs — ¾ cup
  • Parmesan — ¼ cup, grated
  • Egg — 2
  • Garlic — 3 cloves, minced
  • Dried oregano — 1 tsp, salt, pepper
  • Bake at 400°F, 18–20 min. Cool fully, then freeze.

Covers Monday weeks 1 & 3

Overnight oats — 3 jars (Mon · Tue · Wed)
  • Rolled oats — ½ cup per jar
  • Milk — ½ cup per jar
  • Greek yogurt — ¼ cup per jar
  • Honey or maple syrup — 1 tsp per jar
  • Toppings to add in the morning: sliced banana, berries, or granola

Stir, lid on, refrigerate overnight. Grab and go.
