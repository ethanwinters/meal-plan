Sunday Prep
On the grill

Bone-in chicken thighs + big batch: zucchini, bell pepper, red onion

Half the grilled veg is dinner. Half goes in the fridge for quinoa bowls.
Quinoa bowls — Mon · Tue · Wed lunches (3 containers)
  • 1½ cups dry quinoa
  • 1 can cannellini beans, drained
  • Leftover grilled veg (chopped)
  • Lemon-olive oil vinaigrette

Divide into 3 containers. Good for 3 days refrigerated.

2 containers. Keeps 2 days in the fridge.
