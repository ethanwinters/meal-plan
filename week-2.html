Sunday Prep
Air fryer + oven

Chicken thighs (lemon-oregano) + oven-roast batch: cherry tomatoes, zucchini, red onion

Half the veg + one thigh each is dinner. Rest goes in the fridge for rice bowls.
Mediterranean rice bowls — Mon · Tue · Wed lunches
  • 1½ cups dry jasmine rice — cook and cool
  • Air-fried chicken thighs — sliced
  • Roasted cherry tomatoes, zucchini, red onion
  • Kalamata olives
  • Crumbled feta
  • Lemon-olive oil vinaigrette

Divide into 3 containers. Good for 3 days refrigerated.

  • Pesto — 3 tbsp

2 containers. Keeps 2 days in the fridge.
